VOCAL SOMATICS The local vocal tract is MLX Fish Audio S2 Pro, specifically mlx-community fish-audio-s2-pro-8bit. Jarvis normalizes local voice samples into 44.1 kilohertz WAV references, merges the best clips, derives a transcript with the paired MLX speech-to-text model, and then renders cloned speech with the MLX text-to-speech binary. On machines at or below eight gigabytes of RAM, the runtime forces MLX_AUDIO_DEVICE to cpu. At the moment, the local MLX handoff is active as a low-priority background render of the proactive greeting. The remote vocal cord is the NVIDIA Magpie bridge over grpc dot nvcf dot nvidia dot com with the current function route used during recovery. That bridge is now the reliable foreground renderer for startup speech and long-form exports, while Charlie carries transport through LiveKit and the jarvis-voice worker.
