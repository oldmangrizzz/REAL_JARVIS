COGNITIVE CORE The cognitive regulator is the Pheromind engine in JarvisCore. Each directed edge stores pheromone, somatic weight, last-updated time, and success and failure counts. Agent outcomes are reduced to ternary control values: minus one for repel, zero for neutral, and plus one for reinforce. The regulator thresholds are plus or minus 0.2, so noisy scores collapse into deterministic routing pressure instead of agent chatter. The global update is phi of i j t plus one equals one minus epsilon times phi of i j t plus the sum of delta tau deposits on that edge. Delta tau is the signed total of agent deposits. Evaporation is adaptive, not fixed: it starts at 0.12, rises with staleness, rises again with failure bias, and is clamped between 0.05 and 0.95. Somatic weight is reinforced by delta tau times a 0.35 learning rate and never drops below zero, which turns repeatedly successful paths into reflex arcs. Edge selection is greedy on pheromone plus somatic weight.
