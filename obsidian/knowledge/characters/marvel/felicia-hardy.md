# Felicia Hardy / Black Cat

**Publisher:** Marvel Comics · **First appearance:** _The Amazing Spider-Man_ #194 (July 1979)
**Creators:** Marv Wolfman, Dave Cockrum, Keith Pollard
**Origin:** New York City · **Affiliations:** Heroes for Hire (former), independent operator

---

## Summary

Felicia Hardy is a master cat-burglar trained from childhood by her father (also
a thief). Under the Black Cat identity she combines acrobatic skill, psionic
"bad-luck" probability manipulation (in some arcs), and retractable claws. She
has operated as both ally and adversary to Spider-Man, serving as one of his
longest-running romantic interests in Earth-616. Later stories cast her as a
crime boss of the New York underworld, though she frequently returns to
anti-hero / team-up roles.
