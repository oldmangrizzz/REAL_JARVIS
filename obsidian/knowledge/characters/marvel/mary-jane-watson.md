# Mary Jane Watson

**Publisher:** Marvel Comics · **First appearance:** _The Amazing Spider-Man_ #25 (1965, cameo); #42 (1966, full face reveal)
**Creators:** Stan Lee, John Romita Sr. · **Origin:** Queens, New York City

---

## Summary

Mary Jane Watson is Peter Parker's longtime romantic partner and in many
continuities his wife. Originally introduced as the vibrant, self-possessed
"party girl" next door, she has been written in modern canon as a model,
Broadway actress, nightclub owner, and — crucially — one of the few non-powered
characters who knows Peter's secret identity from the outset (later retcons
vary). Her defining posture is civilian agency: she navigates the super-hero
world without becoming its captive.
