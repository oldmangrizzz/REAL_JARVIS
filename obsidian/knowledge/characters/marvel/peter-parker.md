# Peter Benjamin Parker / Spider-Man

**Aliases:** Peter Parker · Spider-Man · Spidey
**Publisher:** Marvel Comics · **First appearance:** _Amazing Fantasy_ #15 (Aug 1962)
**Creators:** Stan Lee, Steve Ditko
**Origin:** Queens, New York City · **Affiliations:** Avengers, Fantastic Four, Daily Bugle

---

## Summary (Earth-616)

Peter Parker, an orphaned teenager raised by his Aunt May and Uncle Ben in
Queens, was bitten by a radioactive spider and gained superhuman strength,
agility, reflexes, a precognitive "spider-sense," and the ability to cling to
surfaces. Initially using his powers for showmanship and profit, he learned the
canonical lesson — _with great power comes great responsibility_ — after a
burglar he declined to stop killed Uncle Ben. He then engineered wrist-mounted
web-shooters and became Spider-Man, balancing high-school and later
college/adult life with a career of low-overhead street-level heroics and
science-adjacent journalism at the _Daily Bugle_.

Key supporting cast: Aunt May, Mary Jane Watson, Gwen Stacy, J. Jonah Jameson,
Harry Osborn; partnerships with Black Cat (Felicia Hardy) and Miles Morales.
