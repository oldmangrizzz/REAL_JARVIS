# Bruce Wayne / Batman

**Publisher:** DC Comics · **First appearance:** _Detective Comics_ #27 (May 1939)
**Creators:** Bill Finger, Bob Kane
**Origin:** Gotham City · **Affiliations:** Justice League, Bat-Family, Wayne Enterprises, Outsiders

---

## Summary

Bruce Wayne is the son of Thomas and Martha Wayne, Gotham City industrialists
whose murder in front of him as a child drove his lifelong oath to fight crime.
Over years of global training in forensics, martial arts, chemistry, stealth,
tactics, and interrogation, he forged the Batman identity: a theatrical,
fear-based counter to Gotham's organized crime. He funds his operation through
Wayne Enterprises, commands a chain of Robins and Batgirls, and is recognized
in DC continuity as the World's Greatest Detective.

He has no superpowers. His arsenal is wealth, intellect, preparation, and an
explicit code against lethal force.

Various film portrayals (Keaton, Bale, Affleck, Pattinson) compress or reshape
the canon. The Nolan trilogy emphasizes the "normal man pushed too far" angle;
Pattinson's _The Batman_ (2022) foregrounds the detective axis. The operator's
