# Lucius Fox

**Publisher:** DC Comics · **First appearance:** _Batman_ #307 (Jan 1979)
**Creators:** Len Wein, John Calnan
**Origin:** Gotham City · **Affiliations:** Wayne Enterprises (CEO), Bat-Family (advisor)

---

## Summary

Lucius Fox is the chief executive and chief technologist of Wayne Enterprises,
a brilliant engineer-businessman who rebuilt the company during Bruce Wayne's
extended absences. In later canon he is explicitly aware of — or at least
complicit with — the Batman operation, supplying the vehicles, materials, and
financial cover that keep the operation running. He is one of very few
characters Bruce trusts with the tech side of the mission.
