# Jason Todd / Red Hood

**Publisher:** DC Comics · **First appearance:** _Batman_ #357 (Mar 1983, original Jason); Red Hood persona debuts in _Batman_ #635 (2005)
**Creators:** Gerry Conway, Don Newton (original); Judd Winick (Red Hood revival)
**Origin:** Gotham City · **Affiliations:** Outlaws, Bat-Family (estranged), formerly Robin

---

## Summary

Jason Todd is the second Robin, adopted by Bruce Wayne after the boy was caught
stealing the Batmobile's tires. He was killed by the Joker in _A Death in the
Family_ (1988) — a death famously decided by reader vote — and later
resurrected via a Lazarus Pit. He returned under the Red Hood identity (the
original alias of the Joker), operating as an antihero willing to use lethal
force against criminals, putting him in direct conflict with Batman's no-kill
code.
