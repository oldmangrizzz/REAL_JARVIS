# Loom — Narrative Time Threads

> "The Loom is where time threads are woven." — [[concepts/Realignment-1218|Realignment §9]]

**Purpose:** This is the **narrative** side of the vault — where events, turning points, and the operator's own biographical throughline are indexed. [[canon/README|Canon]] answers *what is true*. [[codebase/CODEBASE_MAP|Codebase]] answers *how it's built*. The Loom answers **when, why, and what it cost**.

Evidence-corpus role: when the wiki is read under scrutiny (TinCan proceedings, a future red-team audit, or an operator-successor onboarding), the Loom is the thread that lets a reader reconstruct the *sequence* — not just the destination.
